Motions of Volumetric Actuators

6.832: Underactuated Robotics

For a UROP, I was working on evaluating and designing new behaviors for a modular robot system made up of spherical volumetric actuators. Imagine a 2x4x1 system crawling like an inchworm, or a 2x2x2 system rolling. I'm interested in seeing how these robots could potentially be represented in the Drake workspace to evaluate behaviors or perhaps discover new ones.
